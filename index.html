ACCOMMODATION

  • At the Manor

The Manor is a youth-hostel - so you can just stumble upstairs at the end of the evening.

There are more or less enough beds for everyone so let us know when you RSVP if you'd like to stay. We've booked the whole place so no need to 'phone the venue.

Most of the accommodation is in dorm rooms - you can see the rooms here.

As it's not a hotel you'll need to bring your own toiletries, hair-drier etc.

We'd welcome a small contribution to the cost if you are staying at the Manor - it'll go behind the bar.
